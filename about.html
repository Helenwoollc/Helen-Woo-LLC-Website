About

Nearly 40 years ago, John and Helen Woo started their rental property business to provide affordable housing for residents of Pullman.

After John passed away in 1995, Helen continued to run the business until she passed away in 2016.
Their four children continue their parent's legacy and continue to provide housing to a countless number of accomplished WSU undergraduate and graduate students and professionals.
We strive to treat each resident with integrity and respect and respond to each problem as if it were our own with the help our manager, Bill.
As Butch's Approved Cougar Choice Housing, we are committed to keeping our residents in a safe, peaceful, living environment.
We would love the opportunity to serve you.
